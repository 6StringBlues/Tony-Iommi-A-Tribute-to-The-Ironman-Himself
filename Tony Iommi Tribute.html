Tony Iommi: Father of Heavy Metal Guitar

Tony Iommi Playing his Jaydee Custom "Old Boy" SG guitar during Black Sabbath's farewell tour

Fun Facts About This Icon:

  • Born on February 19th. 1948 in Birmingham, England
  • The only child of a working-class couple who owned a local store
  • Met and attended high school with future Black Sabbath bandmate Ozzy Osbourne
  • At age 17, lost his middle and ring finger fingertips on his right hand in a tragic sheet metal cutting accident during his last day of work at a steel mill
  • Became inspired to play guitar again after listening to a record of famous gypsy jazz guitarist Django Reinhardt who like Tony, lost the use of 2 fingers on his fretting hand after a tragic accident.
  • Created his own prosthetic fingertips by melting down plastic Coke bottle caps over his mother's sewing thimble, then molding them onto his fingertips so they fit properly
  • Tuned his guitar down to make plaining less painfull and in the process gave birth to the tone that metal guitar is known for
